November 13, 2021

Hola Olas

The First Official outing of the Rotaract Club of Ulhasnagar. The event was in association with The Mumbai Surf Club. The instructors taught the basics of surfing and the club members were given the opportunity to surf. After which some beach games were organised.

October 30, 2021

Budding Entrepreneurs

Rotaract Club of Ulhasnagar conducted a webinar regarding Entrepreneurial concerns and overcoming from confusion to clarity to be a thriving and prosperous entrepreneur along with Mr. Pavan founder and owner of the Winged Human association.

October 28, 2021

Spiritual Bliss

Rotaract Club of Ulhasnagar organised a yoga session for refreshment of mind. The instructors Neha Badlani & Disha Badlani trained the members about various Yoga asanas and also conducted a meditation session which helped all the members to improvise strength and harmony in the mind and body .

October 24, 2021

Indopak vs GBM

Everyone has seen empty roads and a huge crowd in front of TV shops during India vs Pakistan match. It is a challenge to organise any sort of meeting at that time. Rotaract Club of Ulhasnagar decided to get rotaractors together and have a fun time watching the match followed by a general body meet. Which encouraged members to attend GBM meets.
