September 10, 2021

Morya Facts

A symbol of wisdom and good luck, Lord Ganesha is the most worshiped Hindu God. Regarded as the reliever of all obstacles, Lord Ganesha's photos, idols and other decorative items find a prominent place in most Hindu households. Marking the birth of the Lord of Wisdom is the festival of Ganesh Chaturthi, which is celebrated across the country for 10 days with fervor and grandeur. Rotaract Club of Ulhasnagar decided to celebrate this festival with a special project named Morya Facts, in which they unveilied many facts about Lord Ganesha.

September 19, 2021

Operation 1909 - Installation

An installation ceremony celebrates the end of an era and a beginning of a new year welcoming the new president and the board for a fresh year ahead. Being a revival club, The Rotaract Club of Ulhasnagar celebrated its first installation ceremony on September 19, 2021. Introduction of Incoming president and Official Handover was done by Rotary President and DRR. The Club President then launched the year Theme and official website of club followed by Merchandise and letterhead.
